Privacy Policy

SBOMScope is a fully client-side application. Your SBOM data is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to any external server.

What we collect

Nothing. SBOMScope does not collect, store, or transmit any uploaded or pasted SBOM content.

How data is handled

All file parsing happens in the browser, and there is no backend involved. Files are only read locally and are not uploaded anywhere.

If you have privacy concerns, please review the project repository on GitHub.
